ABOUT

I am a Web Designer / Front End Web Developer, and Visual Artist based on Vancouver Island B.C. I am specializing in front end technologies such as, Wordpress, and creative design which includes UI Design, digital and traditional illustration, and visual art. I am excited to see how all of my skills will work together to help make uniquely designed digital products. I am a, "use both sides of my brain equally" kind of person. This means that I think both creatively, and logically, and this allows me to create beautiful work balanced by creative principles along with the technical side of design and development.

I graduated in 2009 from the two year Interactive Design program at Capilano University and I worked in the industry as a Web Designer & Front End Developer for a number of years. I moved into other fields of work and over the last five years I followed my passion for visual art. In 2021 I had the opportunity to take the Front End Web Development program through Treehouse Learning along with the Web Design & Development program through Literacy Alberni to update my skills to current practices. I have graduated with a Techdegree from Treehouse Learning in Front End Web Development and also from the Literacy Alberni program.

In my spare time you will find me creating fine art, taking walks with my camera for reference photos for my art and design, playing music (I am a Jazz/Blues guitarist), cycling, and spending time with family and my pet hamster Waffles.
