ABOUT

I am a Visual Artist, Web Designer / Front End Developer based on Vancouver Island B.C. I am specializing in front end Technologies, Wordpress, and creative design such as visual art, UI Design, and illustration. I am a, "use both sides of my brain equally" kind of person. This means that I think both creatively, and logically, and this allows me to create beautiful work balanced by creative principles along with the technical side of design and development.

I graduated in 2009 from the two year Interactive Design program at Capilano University and worked in the industry as a web designer & front end developer for a number of years. I moved into other areas for work over time but but in 2021 I had the opportunity to take a program to update my skills to current practice in development and I took it. I have graduated with a Techdegree from Treehouse in Front End Web Development.

In my spare time you will find me creating fine art, taking walks with my camera, playing music, cycling, and spending time with family and my pet hamster Waffles.
