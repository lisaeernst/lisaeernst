Who I Am

I’m a Web Designer / Front End Web Developer, and Visual Artist and I live on Vancouver Island B.C. I specialize in web design and front end development. I love Wordpress, HTML, CSS, JS, designing, accessibility, and digital and traditional illustration.

What I've Done

In 2009 I graduated with honours from the two year Interactive Design program at Capilano University. I worked in the industry as a Web Designer / Developer for a number of years then over time moved into other industries.

What's Happening Today

Shuttle along to 2021 and I have recently finished a nine month long Front End Development Program which includes my Techdegree certificate in Front End Development through Treehouse Learning where I exceeded expectations in all of my projects.

I am happy to say I am up to speed with so many great tools to make beautiful and functional websites and applications!
